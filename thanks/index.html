Thanks & credits

This app bundles artwork and icons from the projects below. We are grateful to their authors and maintainers.

Platform icons

Default platform logos shown in the designer, on NFC card labels, and in the platform color square are adapted from the Carbon EmulationStation theme by RetroPie.

  • RetroPie/es-theme-carbon — system artwork (*/art/system.svg) for each supported console

Game artwork

Box art, title screens, and in-game images are sourced from RetroAchievements and bundled locally for offline use. Game metadata is also drawn from their public API during maintainer setup.

Fonts & UI

The Carbon theme also ships Cabin Bold and other UI assets we do not currently bundle; only the platform system SVGs listed above are used here.
